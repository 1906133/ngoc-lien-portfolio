I am a Senior

I’ve always carried a sense of excitement for new flavors, because for me, every dish is more than just food—it’s a story waiting to be tasted. Each bite, each aroma, and every unfamiliar combination whispers its own lesson. I believe that exploring the world through food is also a way of exploring myself: the more I taste, the more open my palate and my mind become; the more I experience, the deeper I understand the rich and wonderful diversity of life.
